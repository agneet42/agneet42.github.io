Agneet Chatterjee

I am a 1st year PhD student at Arizona State University, where I work at the intersection of computer vision and natural language processing. I am fortunate to be advised by Chitta Baral and Yezhou Yang.

I received my Bachelors in Computer Science from Jadavpur University in 2019, where I worked with Ram Sarkar , Sarbani Roy & Ujjwal Maulik.

I spent 3 years as a software engineer at Salesforce, working in the Quote to Cash domain. I also researched at the CNeRG Lab with Niloy Ganguly, dabbling on social and spatio-temporal graphs. I was an intern at Samsung Research India developing physically based renderers.

Research

My current research interests are in exploring the role of language-guided knowledge augmentation in aiding low-level computer vision tasks. I am also keen on generative models and VL Navigation.
